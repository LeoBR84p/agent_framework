Required Sections Checklist

    DRAFT — NOT PUBLISHED. This template must be reviewed and approved by qualified legal counsel before use. All FILL placeholders must be replaced with jurisdiction-appropriate language.

    1. Service Description

    Describe the service: what it does, who can use it, account requirements, and service scope for each plan tier.

    2. User Obligations

    Define acceptable use policy, prohibited activities, account security obligations, and compliance requirements.

    3. Intellectual Property

    Define ownership: company IP vs user content. License terms in both directions. DMCA process if applicable.

    4. Limitation of Liability

    Draft limitation of liability clauses including warranty disclaimer, damage exclusions, and liability cap. Requires legal review for your jurisdiction.

    5. Termination

    Define termination conditions for both parties, data handling post-termination, refund policy on termination, and which clauses survive.

    6. Data Protection & Privacy (LGPD / GDPR)

    Include LGPD and GDPR compliance statements, reference to privacy_policy.html, and summary of user data rights. For Brazilian and EU users especially.

    7. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

    Specify governing law, jurisdiction, dispute resolution mechanism (arbitration or courts), and standard boilerplate clauses.
